# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis | Power BI Project

## 📌 Overview
This project is an end-to-end data analysis of a pizza sales dataset using **SQL** and **Power BI**.  
The goal was to uncover key business insights around sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

The project goes beyond visualization by focusing on **real business recommendations** derived from the data.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall business performance (revenue, orders, average order value)
- Identify top-selling products and category contributions
- Understand seasonal trends and sales fluctuations
- Analyze customer ordering patterns by time
- Generate actionable business insights and recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Data extraction and transformation  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and visualization  
- **Excel/CSV** – Data storage  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** $817.86K  
- **Total Orders:** 21K  
- **Total Pizzas Sold:** 50K  
- **Average Order Value:** $38.31  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### 1. Sales Overview
- Monthly revenue trends show a **mid-year peak (July)** and a dip in Q4  
- ~12% seasonal variation between highest and lowest months  
- Revenue is **evenly distributed across categories**, reducing dependency risk  

### 2. Product Insights
- Chicken pizzas dominate top-selling items  
- Top 5 pizzas contribute a significant portion of revenue  
- Opportunity to further promote high-performing categories  

### 3. Time-Based Analysis
- **Lunch hours (12–1 PM)** generate ~24% of daily orders  
- Evening (5–7 PM) is the second busiest period  
- Indicates strong demand during specific time windows  

### 4. Size-Based Insights
- Large pizzas generate **~46% of total revenue**  
- Minimal sales in XL/XXL sizes  
- Upselling opportunities exist, especially for certain categories  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Introduce **seasonal promotions** to address revenue dips in Q4  
- Optimize operations during peak hours (especially lunch)  
- Implement **pre-order or express systems** to handle high demand  
- Create **combo deals** during evening hours to increase order value  
- Explore **upselling strategies** for larger pizza sizes
